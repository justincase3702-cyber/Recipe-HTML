Description:

This chocolate cake recipe is rich, moist, and easy to make. Perfect for any occasion, this cake is made with simple ingredients and topped with a creamy chocolate frosting. Enjoy a slice of this decadent dessert that will satisfy your chocolate cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine-inch round pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
