Description:

This is a delicious recipe for biscuits and gravy, a classic comfort food dish. The biscuits are light and fluffy, while the gravy is rich and savory. Best served with eggs and a side of love.

`

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy Dean® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles (or similar sausage of your choice)
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Arrange biscuits 1 to 2 inches apart on an un-greased cookie sheet. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 13 to 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook sausage in a large skillet over medium heat until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently, about 5 to 6 minutes.
  4. Stir in flour until well combined.
  5. Gradually add milk, stirring continuously, until the gravy thickens and comes to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer and stir for 2 more minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  6. Split the warm biscuits in half and ladle the hot sausage gravy over the biscuit halves. Serve immediately.
